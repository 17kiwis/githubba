Duolingo Effectiveness Study

MAIN RESULTS

1. Overall the average improvement in language abilities was 91.4 points and the improvement was statistically significant.

2.The effectiveness measure showed that on average participants gained 8.1 points per one hour of study with Duolingo.

3.The 95% Confidence Interval for the effectiveness is from 5.6 points to 10.7 points gained per one hour of study.

4.The study estimated that a person with no knowledge of Spanish would need between 26 and 49 hours (or 34 hours on average) to cover the material for the first college semester of Spanish. 6.Another factor for higher effectiveness was the initial level of knowledge of Spanish with beginners gaining the most and more advanced learners gaining the least.
